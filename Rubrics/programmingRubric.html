In my class, we focus on theory and technology through practice; you learn via doing. My assignments are designed to give you a fundamental understanding of programming concepts and best practices. Copying code from friends or taking shortcuts will damage the possibility of a career in this field, whether or not you're caught. Passing my classes is not difficult. You need to show up and you need to try. If you do both those things and still need help, I will happily make extra time to ensure you understand what's going on. If it is clear you are phoning it in or shortcutting in your code, I will not be generous with my time.
